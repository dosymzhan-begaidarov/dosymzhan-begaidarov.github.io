My Philosopy

At the heart of my approach to design is a belief that great design is more than just a pretty picture or a cool-looking website. It's about creating solutions that work - that solve problems, meet business goals, and make a real impact on the people who use them.

I take inspiration from Steve Jobs, who famously said: "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."To me, this means that design is not just about aesthetics, but also about function and usability.
